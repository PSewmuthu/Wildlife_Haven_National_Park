    Experience Nature Up Close

    Immerse yourself in the beauty of Wildlife Haven National Park by spending a night under the stars. Whether you prefer tent camping, RV camping, or backcountry camping, we offer a variety of options to suit your needs.

    Tent Camping

    Set up your tent in one of our designated camping areas and enjoy the serenity of nature. Each campsite is equipped with a fire ring and picnic table, providing the perfect setting for a memorable outdoor experience.

    RV Camping

    Bring your RV and enjoy the comfort of home while surrounded by breathtaking scenery. Our RV sites offer electrical hookups and access to water, making it convenient for you to relax and unwind in the great outdoors.

    Backcountry Camping

    For the adventurous spirit, explore our backcountry camping options and discover remote and secluded campsites nestled within the park. Experience the thrill of wilderness camping and create lasting memories as you explore the rugged beauty of Wildlife Haven.

    Reservations

    Advance reservations are recommended for camping at Wildlife Haven National Park. Please check availability and make reservations online or contact our visitor center for assistance.

    Campground Rules

    • Follow Leave No Trace principles and pack out all trash.
    • Respect quiet hours and fellow campers.
    • Observe fire safety guidelines and use designated fire rings.
    • Keep food stored properly to prevent wildlife encounters.
