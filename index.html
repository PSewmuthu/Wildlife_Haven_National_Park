    About Us


    Welcome to Wildlife Haven, an immersive retreat in the heart of Belihuloya. At Wildlife Haven, our mission is to provide a sanctuary where nature thrives, and visitors forge a deep connection with the environment. Our team of passionate environmentalists and park stewards work tirelessly to preserve the natural wonders that define our landscape. Established with a vision of sustainable conservation and environmental education, Wildlife Haven invites you to explore the harmonious balance between adventure and preservation. From meandering trails through ancient forests to panoramic viewpoints overlooking pristine lakes, every inch of Wildlife Haven is a testament to our commitment to ecological integrity. Engage in enriching experiences, from guided nature walks to educational programs that highlight the diverse ecosystems within the park. We believe in fostering a sense of responsibility and respect for the environment, ensuring that every visitor leaves with a greater understanding of the importance of conservation.     Biodiversity Haven

    Wildlife Haven is renowned for its extraordinary biodiversity, housing a diverse range of flora and fauna. The park is home to rare and endangered species, making it a haven for nature enthusiasts and wildlife photographers. Visitors can witness a symphony of bird songs, spot elusive mammals, and discover unique plant species while exploring the well-maintained trails.

    Stargazer's Paradise

    Immerse yourself in the breathtaking celestial display at Wildlife Haven. Away from city lights, the night sky unfolds with a myriad of stars, constellations, and even the occasional meteor shower. The park offers designated stargazing spots and educational programs led by expert astronomers, allowing visitors to marvel at the wonders of the universe in a tranquil and unpolluted environment.

    Adventure for All Seasons

    Wildlife Haven is a year-round destination offering a spectrum of outdoor activities for all seasons. From hiking and camping in the warmer months to snowshoeing and cross-country skiing in winter, the park caters to adventure seekers throughout the year. Each season brings its own charm, ensuring that visitors can experience the beauty of Wildlife Haven in every facet, making it a versatile destination for outdoor enthusiasts.
